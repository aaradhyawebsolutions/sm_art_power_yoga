Transforming Lives Through Yoga & Meditation

Meet Suraj Mokal

Certified Yoga Instructor & Wellness Coach

With over a decade of experience in yoga and holistic wellness, Suraj Mokal has dedicated his life to helping people achieve physical, mental, and spiritual balance through the ancient practice of yoga combined with modern fitness techniques.

Suraj's unique approach combines traditional power yoga with meditation, outdoor activities, and community engagement, creating a comprehensive wellness program that caters to all age groups and fitness levels.

Our Mission

To make yoga and wellness accessible to everyone in the community, regardless of age, fitness level, or background. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the transformative power of yoga and meditation in their daily lives.

Our Vision

To create a healthier, happier community where people of all ages embrace holistic wellness as a way of life. We envision Airoli as a hub of wellness consciousness, where every individual has access to quality yoga instruction and fitness guidance.
